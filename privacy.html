  • Industry standard for safety and compliance

6.1 Deleted Routes Retention

When you delete a route from your dashboard:

  • 6-Month Retention: Deleted routes are kept in our database for 6 months
  • After 6 Months: Routes are permanently deleted from our systems

Why Retain Deleted Routes?

  • Allows recovery if you accidentally delete a route
  • Helps investigate abuse or spam reports
  • Shorter retention than messages (6 months vs 3 years) since routes are public content

If you need a deleted route removed immediately, contact us at hello@goflanerie.com with the route details.
